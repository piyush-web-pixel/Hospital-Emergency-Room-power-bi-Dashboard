# Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard

## Project Title

**Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard**

An interactive **Power BI** dashboard designed to monitor Emergency Room (ER) operations, patient flow, waiting times, admissions, and patient demographics. The dashboard provides healthcare professionals and hospital administrators with actionable insights to improve operational efficiency, optimize resource allocation, and enhance patient care.

---

##  Project Overview

The **Hospital Emergency Room Dashboard** transforms raw hospital data into meaningful visual insights, enabling hospitals to analyze patient volume, admission rates, referral patterns, waiting times, patient satisfaction, and demographic distributions. The dashboard helps decision-makers identify operational bottlenecks and improve healthcare service delivery.

This dashboard is intended for **Hospital Administrators, Healthcare Analysts, Medical Managers, and Decision Makers** who need a centralized view of emergency room performance.

---

##  Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following technologies:

- **Power BI Desktop** – Dashboard development and interactive reporting
-  **Power Query** – Data cleaning, transformation, and preprocessing
-  **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Calculated measures, KPIs, and dynamic calculations
-  **Data Modeling** – Relationships between hospital datasets for accurate analysis
-  **Interactive Visualizations** – KPI Cards, Line Charts, Bar Charts, Donut Charts, and Slicers
-  **File Format** – `.pbix` for development and `.pdf/.png` for presentation

---

## Data Source

**Dataset:** Hospital Emergency Room Patient Records

The dataset contains patient-level emergency room information including:

- Patient ID
- Admission Date
- Patient Age
- Gender
- Race
- Admission Status
- Department Referral
- Patient Satisfaction Score
- Waiting Time
- Patient Critical Medical Condition (CM)

The data enables detailed analysis of patient demographics, hospital operations, and emergency department performance.

## Dashboard Features & Highlights

###  Business Problem

Emergency departments often experience high patient volumes, long waiting times, and limited medical resources. Without a centralized analytics dashboard, it becomes difficult for hospital administrators to monitor operational performance and identify areas requiring immediate attention.

Key questions include:

- How many patients visited the emergency room?
- What percentage of patients were admitted?
- How long do patients wait before receiving treatment?
- Which departments receive the most referrals?
- Which patient age groups visit the ER most frequently?
- What are the demographic patterns of ER patients?
- How satisfied are patients with the services?

---

###  Goal of the Dashboard

The primary objective of this dashboard is to provide a real-time analytical view of Emergency Room performance by:

- Monitoring patient volume and admissions
- Tracking average waiting time
- Measuring patient satisfaction
- Understanding patient demographics
- Identifying department referral trends
- Supporting data-driven healthcare decisions

##  Walkthrough of Key Visuals

###  KPI Cards

The dashboard provides key operational metrics including:

-  Total Patients
-  Total Admissions
-  Average Patient Satisfaction
-  Average Waiting Time
-  Total Department Referrals

These KPIs give hospital administrators an instant overview of emergency room performance.

---

###  Monthly Patient Trend

A line chart displays the monthly number of emergency room visits, helping identify:

- Seasonal patient trends
- Peak admission periods
- Changes in patient volume over time

This supports workforce planning and resource allocation.

---

###  Patient Age Group Analysis

A horizontal bar chart categorizes patients into:

- Child
- Teen
- Adult
- Senior

This helps hospitals understand which age groups require the most emergency care.

---

###  Patient Race Distribution

A bar chart visualizes the number of patients across different racial groups, supporting demographic analysis and healthcare equity assessments.

---

###  Gender Distribution

A donut chart shows the proportion of:

- Male
- Female
- Not Confirmed / Other

This helps understand gender-wise patient distribution in emergency visits.

---

###  Department Referral Analysis

Interactive filters allow users to analyze referrals across departments such as:

- Cardiology
- Neurology
- Orthopedics
- General Practice
- Gastroenterology
- Renal
- Physiotherapy

This enables hospitals to identify departments with the highest patient demand.

---

###  Interactive Filters

Users can dynamically filter the dashboard by:

- Year
- Month
- Department

All visuals update instantly, enabling detailed exploration of emergency room performance.
